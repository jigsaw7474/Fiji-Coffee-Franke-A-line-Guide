General Overview of Franke A-Line Coffee Machines

The Franke A-Line coffee machines (A300, A400, A600, A800) represent cutting-edge technology designed for intensive use in professional environments such as offices, hotels, cafes, and service stations. These machines focus on reliability, exceptional beverage quality, and ease of use, making them a premium choice for high-demand settings.

Key innovations across the A-Line series include the **iQFlow™** technology for optimized extraction and enhanced flavor profiles, the **FoamMaster®** system for consistently perfect hot and cold milk foam, and advanced automatic cleaning systems like **CleanMaster®** and **EasyClean®** for superior hygiene and convenience. The intuitive touchscreens with multilingual interfaces further simplify operation for staff and self-service customers alike.

Cleaning Procedures

All Franke machines include step-by-step touchscreen prompts to guide users through daily and weekly cleaning tasks. Below is a model-specific summary.

Franke A300

Daily:

  1. Menu → Cleaning & Maintenance → Clean Coffee Machine
  2. Rinse cycle runs for coffee circuit
  3. Clean grounds bin, drip tray, and milk spout

Weekly:

  1. Remove spout/brew parts → soak in hot water + detergent
  2. Milk system: Clean using Franke Milk Cleaning agent
  3. Descale monthly depending on water quality

Franke A400

Daily:

  • Rinse all product lines via menu
  • Wipe display, handles, nozzles with microfiber cloth

Weekly:

  1. Run CleanMaster full clean (automated)
  2. Manual cleaning: remove brew group, soak, and brush
  3. Clean milk lines using approved solution and rinse thoroughly

Franke A600

Daily:

  • Coffee and milk system auto rinse at shutdown
  • Clean drip tray, grounds container, exterior touch surfaces

Weekly:

  1. Full CleanMaster cycle
  2. Remove and clean foam nozzles, milk lines, iced-coffee attachment
  3. Chocolate dispenser cleaning if applicable

Monthly:

  • Descale boilers and hot water lines
  • Inspect gaskets, connectors, and clean grinder housing

Franke A800

Daily:

  1. Rinse via maintenance menu (coffee/milk/chocolate)
  2. Remove and wipe powder hoppers, brew spouts
  3. Clean touch display, cooler, and steam wand

Weekly:

  1. EasyClean deep cycle: Cleans milk, flavor, chocolate lines
  2. Brew unit removal → soak and sanitize → reassemble
  3. Clean coin/payment modules, suction hoses

Monthly:

  • Deep descaling (with certified agent)
  • Clean air filter, rear vents, and internal fans
  • Perform system health check via UI diagnostics

Parts & Modules Summary

  • Brew Unit: Removable in all models except A300 (semi-fixed).
  • Grinders: Ceramic or steel options; A800 supports parallel grinding.
  • Milk Systems: FoamMaster® enables hot/cold foam in different textures.
  • Chocolate/Flavor Units: Optional modules; cleaned via automated cycles. Require separate weekly manual clean (hoses and tanks).
  • Cooling Units: SU05/SU12 coolers support 1-2 milk types. Cleanable drip tray, replaceable suction hose, and washable seals.
  • Touch Display: 8" or 10.4" touchscreen (model dependent). Multilingual interface and cleaning wizards included.

Important Notes for Installation and Maintenance:

  • Use only Franke-certified cleaning agents to prevent damage or voiding warranty.
  • Set cleaning reminders based on beverage output volume.
  • Maintain water quality logs if used in a HACCP-compliant environment.
  • For installations, ensure:
    • Drain pipe slope 22%
    • Easy access to water shutoff
    • Clear ventilation behind the unit
